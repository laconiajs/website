Support Me

Laconia doesn't have a big company behind that sits behind it. Laconia is an Apache-licensed open source project, and is a result of the huge time commitment, working before and after hours. I fully rely on the support of people and community. As I'm motivated to create something that's useful for people, I'm finding it helpful to get any kind of feedback, which you can do by any of the following:

  • Be involved in the community! Feel free to chat or ask questions at gitter
  • If you find something wrong, missing, or might be helpful, feel free raise an issue or pull request in GitHub. What's helpful for is helpful for other people!
  • If you love the project, give it a star!
  • If you're using Laconia, drop a hello on Twitter or email me at wisen[at]ceilfors.com
